Return Policy - Track & Field


1.Returns and Exchanges:

We accept returns and exchanges for unopened and unused sports equipment and merchandise within 7 days of the purchase date. Please contact our customer support team for assistance.


2.Refund Process:

Refunds for returned sports equipment and merchandise will be processed within 7 business days after we receive the unopened and unused items. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.


3.Damaged or Defective Items:

If your order arrives damaged or with defects, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
